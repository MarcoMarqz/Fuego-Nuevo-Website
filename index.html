Our Mission

Fuego Nuevo is commited to preserving and sharing the cultural tradtition of Mexican Folklorico dance. Through Perfomance, teachings, and community engagement, we encourage dancers of all backgrounds to embrace the beauty of expression.

What We Do

PERFORMANCES
We showcase traditional Folklorico dances from a variety of Mexican states, bringing culture to life at community events, festivals, and celebrations.
CLASSES
We offer classes for dancers of all levels, from brand new to experienced. We provide practice that builds confidence, technique, and passion.
