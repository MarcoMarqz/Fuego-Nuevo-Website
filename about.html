ABOUT US

Fuego Nuevo USA is a non-profit organization dedicated to preserving and promoting Mexican Folklorico dance and culture within the United States.

Fuego Nuevo USA has been a community hub for individuals passionate about Mexican traditions, dance, and music. The organization offers dance classes, workshops, and cultural events that celebrate the rich heritage of Mexico. Through performances and educational programs, Fuego Nuevo USA aims to inspire a deeper understanding and appreciation of Mexican culture among diverse audiences. The organization is committed to nurturing talent, building community, and ensuring that the vibrant traditions of Mexican folk dance continue to thrive in the U.S.

What is Folklorico?

Folklorico is a traditional Mexican style of dance that draws from history, customs, and stories of each region of Mexico. Each state has its own unique music, costumes, and choreographic style.

States We Highlight

Jalisco
Known for its vibrant and colorful costumes, Jalisco folklorico features lively footwork and energetic movements. The dance often includes the use of wide skirts for women and charro suits for men.
