Monthly Payment

A monthly contribution of $15 is requested to support the dance group funding needed for necessities like vestuario, transportation, and trips. Our goal is to make this form of expression accessible to everyone, so if payment is a concern, please contact us to discuss a possible solution. We want anyone who wishes to join to be able to do so!

Join the Community

Being part of Fuego Nuevo is about more than just dance, it's about connecting with others, celebrating culture, and sharing joy. Whether through contributing, volunteering, or simply attending our performances, your involvement makes a difference and strengthens our community.

Ready to Join?
